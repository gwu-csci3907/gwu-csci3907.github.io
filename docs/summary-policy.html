Class Policy on Collaboration

  • Your instruction will address HW-related questions for up to one-week after the due date.
  • You may study in groups, but you may not discuss HWs among yourselves.
  • Each student is expected to work out the HW programs independently.
  • Under no circumstances may you look at another student’s HWs.
  • You are encouraged
    • to discuss the class material on the course website and
    • post your queries on slack for your instructors and peers to comment
  • You are not allowed to
    • discuss HW problems nor give out hints to the HW problems or
    • provide hints or suggestions on slack or
    • discuss projects, tests, exams and quizzes, unless explicitly assigned a group quiz or a group HW.
      • NOTE: You will be given opportunities to work in groups
    • use chatGPT or other generate AI tools to complete your HWs, projects, tests, exams and quizzes unless explicity permitted by the instruction team
Important

All violations will be treated as violations of the Code of Academic Integrity

Justice, Equity, Diversity, Inclusion (JEDI)

  • It is our intention that this course serve the needs of all students who have the prerequisites.
  • We believe that the diversity of background and of perspective among the students and the instructional team is an asset.
  • We will strive to present material that is respectful of the diverse backgrounds and characteristics of the student body and the instructional team.
  • Each of us, whether student or instructional team member, is expected to treat all others with respect. Disrespect in any form will not be tolerated.
  • Please contact Bulusu with any concerns or suggestions.
